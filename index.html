Flip 7 — Official Rules

Deck: Numbers 0–12 where count = value (twelve 12s, one 1, one 0), plus Score Modifiers (+2,+4,+6,+8,+8,+10,×2) and Action cards.

Each Round: Dealer deals one card to every player, then players take turns clockwise choosing to Flip or Bank.

Bust: Draw a duplicate number = 0 points for the round.

Flip 7: Collect 7 different numbers → round ends for all. Everyone who hasn't busted banks. Flip 7 player gets +15 bonus.

Action Cards:

  • Freeze — Forces a target active player to bank.
  • Flip Three — Forces a target active player to flip 3 cards.
  • Second Chance — Held in hand, saves you from one bust.

Score Modifiers: +cards add flat points; ×2 doubles your number total first.

Win: First to 200 points wins.
